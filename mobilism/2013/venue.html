Conference venue

The Mobilism 2013 conference will take place in Pathé Tuschinski, a beautiful movie theatre in the heart of Amsterdam.

Address

Pathé Tuschinski
Reguliersbreestraat 26-34
1017 CN Amsterdam
View on Google Maps

Workshops venue

The workshops will take place in Felix Meritis, a venerable 200-year-old venue that was originally a club for well-to-do 18th century science aficionados, then became the headquarters of the Dutch Communist Party for a while, and then a theatre and a conference venue.

Both the 'Kremlin' and 'Koepelzaal' rooms will be used by us that day.

Address

Felix Meritis
Keizersgracht 324
1016 EZ Amsterdam
