Mobilism 2012 is done

We'll be back in 2013

During the conference we'll give away the following devices from our sponsors:

  • 10 BlackBerry PlayBook tablets
  • 8 Nokia Lumia 800 phones with Windows Phone
  • 2 Nokia N9 phones with MeeGo
  • 3 Nokia Asha 303 phones with S40
  • 10 Samsung Galaxy Nexus phones with Android 4 from Google
  • 10 HP TouchPad tablets with webOS

Each attendee can win only one device. We'll randomly select attendees who are allowed to draw a token with the device they've won. The actual devices will be handed out in exchange for the token later on. We'll announce the exact procedure during the conference. Speakers, sponsors, and organisers are excluded from winning devices.
