Mobilism 2015

27th of March, Amsterdam

Tickets on sale now

22 January 2013 路 Krijn

We've only got 30 15 early-bird tickets left, and they will run until January 25th. If you didn't make up your mind yet, perhaps this update will. This year we're going back to a single track conference, no fast track like last year. In total we'll have 16 sessions lined up for you, including our mobile browser panel, hosted by Jeremy Keith. We'll also have deep diving talks about UX, content strategy, the responsive images rathole, mobile rendering performance, console browsers, some case studies, mobile JS APIs, and more. Today we're adding three more names to our programme.

Both Dave Shea and Derek Featherstone are very well known for their work on the web. They've both, obviously, moved to mobile and want to share their experience with us. Dave will talk about mobile design anti-patterns and Derek will discuss accessibility options on mobile.

Our MC this year will be Tim Kadlec! Formerly one of the driving forces behind the popular Breaking Development conferences in the USA, author and now an independent consultant. In between sessions he will be responsible for all Q&A in an interview-style with the speakers.

If you've made up your mind for the 16th and 17th of May, please join us. If you already did so, thanks! You won't be disappointed.

We're looking forward to our third edition, and we hope to see you there!
