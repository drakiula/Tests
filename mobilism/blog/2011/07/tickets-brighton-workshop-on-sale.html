Tickets on sale now

19 July 2011 · PPK

Just a quick note to say that ticket sale for the Mobilism workshop in Brighton on 9th and 10th of November is now open. PPK will run the Brighton workshop by himself; Stephen can't make it.

A ticket costs £595, and as usual it gives you two days of workshoppy goodness plus lunches plus dinner on day 1. As a nice extra, the good people at LeftLogic have decided to also give you a Full Frontal ticket for free. Now that's value for money!

Of course you can also buy tickets for our Amsterdam or Frankfurt workshops. Tickets for the Manchester one will become available later.

See you in one of these cities!
