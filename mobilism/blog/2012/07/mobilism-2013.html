Mobilism 2015

27th of March, Amsterdam

Tickets on sale now

19 July 2012 · Krijn

The third edition of Mobilism will take place on 16th and 17th of May, 2013, in Amsterdam. Just like the previous two, we'll invite top speakers from the web and mobile worlds to educate and entertain you about the mobile web.

Mobilism 2012 and 2011 were a blast. If you don't believe us, take a look at the videos yourself. We expect to be able to maintain the high standard we set.

Ticket sales will start in November. We expect tickets to be around €600. We're going to arrange workshops on the day before the conference, Wednesday 15th of May. The venue will be the same as in 2012.

See you in May in Amsterdam!
