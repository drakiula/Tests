Anna Debenham and Max Firtman announced; ticket sales open 路 News

Mobilism 2015

27th of March, Amsterdam

Tickets on sale now

28 November 2012 路 PPK

Today we're very glad to be able to announce two more Mobilism 2013 speakers:

  • Anna Debenham, who amazed the world by her great work on game console browsers. Of course we just HAD to get her to speak at Mobilism, and to our relief she graciously agreed.
  • Max Firtman, who has been working on the mobile web for many a year now, developing for BlackBerry and Nokia back when most web developers didn't think about mobile at all. He also wrote several books on the topic.

Also, we opened our ticket sales. Early bird tickets are available, and we have a special offer for our most loyal visitors. See our ticket page for the details.

See you in May!
